    LA Stone Therapy

    Hot stone massage combines the benefits of thermotherapy with massage strokes, creating a deeper and more relaxing treatment.

    Polished and smooth, water- heated Basalt (lava rocks) stones are used to apply pressure and heat to the body. Stones coated in oil can also be used to deliver various massaging strokes. The stones are placed along the client’s back; they help to retain heat which then deeply penetrates into the muscles.

    Hot stone massage can:

    • Boost your circulation
    • Release stored tension
    • Recharge your energy levels
    • Relax you

    Hot stone massage is said to have health benefits for people with:

    • Muscular pain
    • Poor circulation
    • Rheumatic and arthritic conditions
    • Fibromyalgia
    • Back pain
    • Stress
    • Insomnia
    • Depression
